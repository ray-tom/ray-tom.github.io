Punching

Punching - your exclusive mission management tool


Features:

1. Add tasks: The task attributes are various, you can choose: the day of the week to complete the task, the task end date, the day reminder time, the task category.

2. Punch every day: See how many tasks you have not completed today? Come and make a card, just tap it and record it. You can also choose a different date to see how the mission completed on that day.

3. Task Management: Your Task Manager can be sorted by multiple attributes, and each task can be edited again. You can sort tasks by day of the week and task categories to improve efficiency.

4. Calendar Statistics: Click on a task to see which days you have not completed? If you accidentally forget it, just click on the corresponding date and make a punch card once! If the day does not happen for some reason, you can skip the punch. The days of skipping do not affect the progress.

5. Notification reminder: When the task is completed!

6. Widget plugin: Quickly view today's unfinished tasks in the Widget plugin and quickly punch the card.
